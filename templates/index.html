Music Through the Years

Music has been around for a long time and has been created by all cultures. In America, there are endless varieties of music that we listen to on a daily basis.

The purpose of this project is to analyze what artists have been popular and how music has changed though the years. So, we are using Billboardâ€™s Hot 100 from 1960 to 2018 to analyze popular music over the last 60 years.

The Billboard Hot 100 is has long been the standard in the music industry weekly and yearly rankings. While the method has changed over time, a song is currently ranked by sales (physical & digital), radio play and online streaming in the US.
