Music Through the Years

Music has been around for a long time and has been created by all cultures. In America, there are so many different kinds of music that we listen to daily.

So, we are analyzing Billboard’s Music from 1960 to 2018. The Billboard Hot 100 is today’s music industry standard record chart in the United States for weekly and end of the year ranking songs. How does a song rank by: sales (physical & digital), radio play and online streaming in the US. The purpose of this project is to analyze how music has changes over the past years. Music fans are very curious to see if their favorite artists or songs made the list. Therefore, we have a couple of discoveries that viewers are very anxious to see.
